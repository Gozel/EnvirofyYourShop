The Envirofy Project

Envirofy is the first science-based web browser extension that supports online shoppers in making sustainable food choices. The Envirofy addon is part of a multi-disciplinary research project from the Univeristy of Glasgow and the Univeristy of Bristol. The goal of our research is to determine which behaviour change techniques are the most effective in supporting online shoppers to buy sustainable food products.
