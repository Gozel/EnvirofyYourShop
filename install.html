Installation

The Envirofy addon is part of a research project at the Univeristy of Glasgow and the Univeristy of Bristol. The aim of our research is to determine which behaviour change techniques support consumers in buying eco-friendly foods. You can read up on the techniques we use to support you, or watch a short demo video.

Envirofy works with Waitrose and Tesco.
