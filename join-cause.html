Join the Cause

Do you want businesses and world leaders to act on the climate crisis? Install Envirofy now. Envirofy's algorithm not only helps you to lower your carbon footprint effortlessly; if used on a large scale, it will change suppliers' behaviour. In order to improve their ratings and visibility in Envirofy's interface, suppliers will have to produce more sustainable products.

We communicate the results from our research with our collaborators at the Food Standards Agency - a governmental body that regulates food businesses. The more people use Envirofy, the stronger a point we can make in influencing food policies towards sustainability.

If you have ideas and feedback on how to improve Envirofy, please do not hesitate to contact us on info@envirofy.eu.

Follow us on Twitter, LinkedIn, and Facebook to connect with like-minded peers and stay informed about anything Envirofy related.
